💡 Quick Instructions: How to Use the Cart View

  • 🟢 Green Slots (Normal Operations): Shows the name of your pre-assigned permanent student for this class period. No scanning or typing needed!
  • 🔄 Handle Exceptions (Click Slots): Click a slot to toggle its status. Safely log **Temporary Borrowers** or **Overnight Loans** without messing up your master class rosters.
  • 📅 Change Periods & Rosters: Use the class period dropdown to instantly rotate names. Head to the **Rosters & Dashboard** tab to edit permanent seat assignments.
